Currently I’m trying to capture some mysterious animals. They look like apparitions of horses, but with deformed bodies. Some of them may have twisted necks, fragmented heads, or almost horse-like appearance but with disturbing glitches. They are inspired by a computer algorithm designed to generate fake images of reality, which succeeds in mimicking human faces, cats, or artworks, yet often malfunctions when making horses. Though horses are powerful and well-formed animals of masculine glory, these monsters that fascinate me are abnormal. They are horses that do not exist.
