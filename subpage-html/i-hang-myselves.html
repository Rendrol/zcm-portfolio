I Hang Myselves When I Desire to be a Beauty

In Death and the Maiden, Elfriede Jelinek wrote:

"I'm more durable than other objects for I'm both flesh and blood and not flesh and blood...

I'm made of this or that style of dresses, coats or jumpsuits... I am different types of cloth".
